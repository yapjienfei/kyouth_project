⚠️ Limitations & Caveats

Data sources & freshness:

  • Job listings sourced from a static snapshot of ~300 Malaysian IT job postings (2025). Salary data reflects a point-in-time snapshot; actual market rates may differ.
  • Freshness indicators show "0 months old" as this is the initial dataset load. Click Re-validate to simulate a refresh check.
  • AI replaceability scores are derived from WEF Future of Jobs Report 2025 mappings and Gemini-generated analysis — they represent probabilistic risk estimates, not certainties.

Methodology:

  • Skill extraction uses Gemini AI with a controlled taxonomy. False positives/negatives may occur, especially for niche skills.
  • Career paths are computed via BFS on a graph where edges require skill transition cost < 80% and upward salary trajectory. Paths reflect average listings, not individual company requirements.
  • Salary delta is computed from median values across all matching job listings. Roles with undisclosed salary are excluded from delta calculations.
  • Confidence scores combine data density, recency (mock: 0 days since refresh), and skill gap ease. They should not be interpreted as hiring probability.

Limitations:

  • Does not account for years of experience, education, soft skills, or location preferences.
  • Market trends are mock values for illustration; real trend data requires integration with live job board APIs.
  • Learning time estimates are AI-generated approximations; individual learning pace varies significantly.
